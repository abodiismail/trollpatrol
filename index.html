Privacy Policy

How We Collect
Information

We collect information to provide the Muzzle service: giving you power to make the Internet a human friendly place. We collect information from data your browser sends us. As most other sites, we also use "cookies" to understand how you use Muzzle, and to improve our service so it best fits your needs.

How We Use
Information

Through the information you provide us, we are able to identify the areas of issue and address them. Furthermore, we communicate with you and about the things you care to know about. Changes To
Policy

Muzzle is a project by 4 data geeks. We are always looking for new ways we can use the information you provide us to create greater value for you. This means our policy will undergo changes as we develop Muzzle. Information will be governed by the policy that is in place in the time of its collection.
